Research Synopsis

📄 IEEE FORMAT · SYNOPSIS

Semiconductor Materials for Emerging & Integrated Technologies

Arnav Pandita · Dept. of E&TC, Mumbai University (TSEC, India)

Abstract — Semiconductor technologies play a major role in modern electronics, artificial intelligence, IoT systems, and communication devices. This research synopsis focuses on semiconductor materials and emerging integrated technologies that support low-power, high-speed, and intelligent electronic systems. The study explores the importance of semiconductor materials in next-generation communication and embedded applications while emphasizing energy efficiency, miniaturization, and advanced integration.

Key Objectives:
  • Study semiconductor materials in emerging integrated technologies
  • Understand nanoscale fabrication and integration techniques
  • Explore low-power semiconductor applications in communication & embedded systems
  • Analyze the role of semiconductors in AI and intelligent hardware systems
